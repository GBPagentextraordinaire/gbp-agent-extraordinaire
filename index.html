GBP Agent Extraordinaire

The Local SEO Audit Agent That Works Where You Do

What it does

GBP Agent Extraordinaire is a free Chrome extension that extracts, analyses, and exports Google Business Profile data in real time. Open any business listing on Google Maps or Search, click the floating audit button, and get an instant breakdown of everything that matters for Local SEO.

Key features

  • Category Detection: Instantly see primary and secondary GBP categories for any business—no guessing, no manual lookups. Categories are visually tagged and colour‑coded so you can spot primary vs. secondary at a glance.
  • Full Business Profile Extraction: Name, address, phone, website, business hours, attributes, photo count— all pulled into one clean panel. Every field is copyable with a single click.
  • Business Identifiers: CID, Place ID, and GPS coordinates extracted automatically. Essential for citation building, API lookups, and advanced Local SEO workflows.
  • Review Analysis: See total review count, average rating, and star distribution. Run a deeper analysis on visible reviews to get keyword frequency data, Local Guide counts, and photo review stats— invaluable for understanding what customers are saying about competitors.
  • One‑Click Data Export: Export full business audit data as CSV, export reviews as CSV, or download everything as JSON. Copy NAP (Name, Address, Phone) to your clipboard instantly for citation consistency checks.
  • Quick Links: Direct links to Google PageSpeed Insights, Mobile‑Friendly Test, CID‑based Maps links, and search results—all generated from the business data, saving you time on every audit.
  • Audit History: The extension remembers your recent audits so you can quickly revisit businesses you've already analysed.

Who it's for

  • Local SEO professionals and agencies auditing client and competitor GBPs
  • Digital marketers running competitive analysis on Google Maps
  • Business owners who want to understand how their profile compares to competitors
  • Citation builders who need accurate NAP data fast
  • Anyone who works with Google Business Profiles regularly

How to use it

  1. Install the extension.
  2. Go to Google Maps or Google Search.
  3. Open any business listing.
  4. Click the teal floating button (bottom‑right corner) or use the extension popup.
  5. Browse the tabbed audit panel: Overview, Categories, Reviews, Export.
  6. Copy, export, or analyse—whatever your workflow needs.

Supported regions

Works on google.com, google.co.nz, google.com.au, google.co.uk, and google.ca — with more domains coming.
